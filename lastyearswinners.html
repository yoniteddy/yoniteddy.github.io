Congrats to Philly's Best Cheesesteaks for winning our 2015 Food Truck Wars Competition!!! It was a hard fought competition down to the last inch of food ... However, Philly's Best Cheesesteaks prevailed.

Where Are They Now?

3

Since the competition in July 2015, business has boomed at Philly's Best Cheesesteaks! They have excelled tremendously, using the prize money to open up a shop. The publicity has resulted in their shops being one of the busiest lunch spots in town. And who do they have to thank? FOOD TRUCK WARS!!!
